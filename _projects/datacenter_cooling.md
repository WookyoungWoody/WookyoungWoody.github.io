## Jet-Enhanced Immersion Cooling

Development of high-efficiency jet-impingement immersion cooling technology for data centers.

### Key Achievements
- Designed and tested immersion cooling systems with real CPU/GPU servers
- Developed integrated thermal management to reduce PUE
- Designed direct liquid cooling (DLC) systems for high-heat chips (CPU/GPU/RAM/SSD)

### Technical Capabilities
- Immersion cooling test rigs for server-level systems
- High-heat chip (CPU/GPU) stress test apparatus design & fabrication
- Dielectric fluid-based thermal performance testing

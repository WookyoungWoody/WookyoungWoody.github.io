## Compact Heat Exchanger for Liquid Hydrogen

Design technology for PCHEs operating below -200°C at 100 MPa for liquid hydrogen vaporization.

### Key Achievements
- Lab-scale PCHE design using SUS 316L plates with multiple channel configurations
- Thermal resistance models and Nusselt number correlations
- Freezing phenomena investigation in cryogenic PCHEs (down to -220°C)
- Supercritical hydrogen/nitrogen test facility operation
- PCHE design software development (Python)

### Technical Capabilities
- 700 bar high-pressure heat exchanger structural design
- 2000 bar pressure test facility operation
- PCHE diffusion bonding fabrication
- Cryogenic heat exchanger performance testing

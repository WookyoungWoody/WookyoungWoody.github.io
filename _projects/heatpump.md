## Advanced Heat Pump Systems

Research on chemisorption heat pumps, low GWP refrigerant systems, and high-temperature heat pumps (300°C class).

### Key Achievements
- Adsorption/chemisorption reactor bed design with optimized sorbent packing
- COP > 1.3 for waste-heat-driven heat pump systems
- VLE measurement apparatus for next-gen low-GWP refrigerant blends
- Falling-film evaporator design for industrial applications
